About Us

ChatCast is a global platform that empowers people to share their voices, stories, struggles, and expertise. By chatting with our AI assistant via WhatsApp, users can effortlessly create professional-quality videos enriched with intros, topic-relevant footage, captions, and interactive QR codes that link to games or quizzes. Our platform simplifies content creation and allows anyone to connect with global audiences by turning everyday conversations into impactful multimedia experiences.

Our Mission

At ChatCast, our mission is to democratize podcasting and video creation by giving everyone the tools they need to share their stories. We believe in empowering individuals from all walks of life to cast their voices without barriers like costly equipment or complex production processes. Everyone deserves a platform to inspire and engage the world.
